How To Book American Airlines Flights?

Confirming the reservations with American Airlines isn't a complicated process. Furthermore, the availability of online and offline ways makes the booking process even more easy.

In order to confirm your ticket through American Airlines official Site, follow the procedures given below.

Online Method

  • Firstly, head to the official website of the American airlines.
  • Select the trip type. Choose from the Round-trip and one-way options.
  • Here, go ahead to the total number of flyers.
  • In this step, mention your travel dates from the calendar given here.
  • Click on search and then choose your preferred flight option from the list that appears here.
  • At last, pay the confirmation amount.
  • The airline will then send the American Airlines flight tickets via the mail.

Offline Method

  • The offline method to finalize your tickets includes speaking with the travel representatives through the phone number.
  • Head to the official website of American airlines and search the contact information.
  • Dial it to get in touch with the travel team.
  • Furthermore, proceed by sharing the travel details like the total number of passengers, date of travel, number of passengers, etc.
  • Finally, choose the flight and make the payment for it.
  • Lastly, the airline will send the American Airlines Flights ticket.

How to Manage American Airlines Booking?

Did you realize the need to make changes to your current reservation? Well, no worries. Travelers can make modifications and finalize them in no time through the American Airlines Manage Booking option.

Refer to the steps given below and follow them in order to confirm the needed changes to your American airline booking.

  • First and foremost, open the American airline official website.
  • Now, click on the "My Trips" tab.
  • Here, mention the booking confirmation number in addition to the passengers last name.
  • In this step, click on "Find reservation".
  • Continue and make the required changes to your booking.
  • Finalize the modifications made here.
  • The changes will apply to theAA Booking in no time.
